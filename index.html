Safety Protocol

While we will not require swab tests for our guests, we will strictly implement the following throughout the ceremony and celebration:

  1. Before the event: if you experience and covid related symptoms (i.e. Fever, Cough, Headache, Sore-throat and the likes), please do not attend the event for the safety of others and let us know before hand, as much as it hurts us not to see you on our wedding day, we will understand as it will be for the safety of the majority.

  2. Before the event: if you happen to have a close contact with a COVID-19 positive patient for the past 14 days, please do not attend the event for the safety of others and let us know immediately. Though it may be too much to ask, we will greatly appreciate if everyone can stay at home for at least 14 days before the wedding to ensure no exposure to the virus.

  3. Always wear your face mask and face shield as you roam around the venue.

  4. Kindly refrain from frequently transferring seats/tables during the event.
